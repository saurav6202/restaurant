Our Story

Founded in 2015, Savory began as a small bistro with a big dream - to revolutionize dining through creative flavors and exceptional service. What started as a 10-table restaurant has now grown into one of the city's most celebrated culinary destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: source the finest ingredients, let their natural flavors shine, and present them with artistic flair. We believe dining should be an experience that engages all the senses.

Chef Maria Rodriguez

Head Chef & Founder
